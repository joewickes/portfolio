I'm a full-stack Web Developer in Tampa, Florida with experience in React, Node.js, and PostgreSQL. I'm currently in coding bootcamp (a practical and fast-paced way to learn how to code), looking to graduate in early March!

ABOUT ME

No other time in history has had such an interconnected world available at its fingertips. I think the web is incredible because it provides a platform in which people can imagine, build, and refine things together. In my opinion, there is no better place to do that from than as a full-stack Web Developer.

Since the internet is built on interconnectivity, it makes a lot of sense that the teams that work on it should do the same. I look forward to the chance to work with other developers so that we can bounce ideas off of one another. When one person gets stuck, someone else can step in to help.

Outside of developing, I love to spend time with my family. I am also really into movies, coffee (Chemex/pour over recently), video games, and spending time outdoors. Since I live in Tampa, I like to go to the beach when I can.

I am a full-stack Web Developer, which can mean one thing to one person, and a totally different thing to another. For me, being a full-stack web developer means that I can build what you see and interact with on a Web Page or Web App, and also how the data behind that is stored more long term.
